Pandas

Why Pandas?

Pandas are beautiful creatures, they are super cute and lazy. They spend most of the day sleeping or eating bamboos and on top of that, they are very clumsy animals. It's hard to find an animal more adorable. I don't know about you, but I can relate to them too, which only makes it even better. Their colors too, I love it.
